# Convolution
- mathematical operation on two functions ($f$ and $g$) that produces a third function ($h=f\cdot g$), representing how the shape of one is modified by the other
$$
f \cdot g = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x-t)g(x) dt
$$
